Vegan Mango Smoothie

The Mango Lovers' Dream

Mango lovers will love this smoothie due to its silky, smooth yet fruitful taste!

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh mangos
  • 1 cup frozen mangos
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1 cup of your favourite juice

Steps

  1. Cut fresh mangos into bite sized pieces
  2. Add fresh mangos, frozen mangos and ice into the blender
  3. Pour juice into blender
  4. Blend until texture is smooth and that there are no chunks
  5. Enjoy!
